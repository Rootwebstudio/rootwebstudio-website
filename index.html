FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build my website?

+
The Basic plan takes 2–3 days and the Standard plan takes a week. We work fast without cutting corners. You'll receive your site files via email or we help you deploy it directly.

Do I need to pay for hosting separately?

+
No! We build static websites that can be hosted for free on platforms like Netlify, Vercel, or GitHub Pages. We guide you through it step by step. You only pay for a domain if you want a custom one (usually ₹1000–₹2000/year).

Can I request changes after delivery?

+
Yes! The Standard plan includes 1 free revision round. For Basic, small text/color changes are included. Additional revisions or design changes can be requested at a small fee.

Will my website work on mobile?

+
Absolutely. Every single site we build is mobile-first. We test on phone screens before delivery. Most of your visitors will be on mobile, and your site will look perfect.

What information do you need from me?

+
Just the basics — your business name, what you do, any logo or images you have, your contact number, and a rough idea of the look you want. We'll take it from there.
